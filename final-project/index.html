Get Over The 1 Week Phase

-Overveiw-

Minecraft offers a vast, open world where you can unleash your creative desires. You can build anything from dirt shacks to grand cities, explore endless generated worlds. The game's simplicity and endless possibilities make it both relaxing, as well as a rewarding experience. Staying in a world allows you to immerse yourself in your creations, track your progress, and form a deeper connection to your virtual home. Minecraft provides a unique and utterly enjoyable way to spend your time. Over the years, Minecraft has sold more than 300 million copies making it the most wide spread game IN THE WORLD. When it comes to this blocky game, one common trend is the “1 week phase”. Gamers return to this game, with intents to create a lasting playthrough. Although, after a week of playing. That ambition is lost and they move on, unfulfilled. Given some time that cycle then repeats itself. Again, over and over. It sucks to not achieve your aspirations. This drives people away from minecraft. This website is here to declare that those creative ambitions with minecraft are possible. That massive base you want to build…IT POSSIBLE. That ginormous redstone farm.. POsSIBLE!! This website is dedicated to both new and old players. It gives tips on how to keep your world fresh and to help maintain that ambition. Let's break that 1 week phase and find true fulfillment out of the greatest game to be ever created.
